Introduction

StudyMate ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with South African data protection laws (including POPIA) and international standards.

Information We Collect

We collect personal data directly from you when you register, use our features, or contact us. This includes:

  • Full name, email address, and password (for account creation)
  • Study tasks, documents uploaded, quiz results
  • IP address, browser type, device info (for security and analytics)
  • Any messages or feedback you provide via our contact form

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide core features (AI summaries, study tracking, quizzes)
  • Improve user experience and platform performance
  • Analyze trends and track engagement (anonymously)
  • Send updates, notifications, and support responses

AI & Document Processing

When you upload study material, it is temporarily processed by AI to generate summaries and quizzes. Your content is not stored permanently or shared with third parties.

Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies to:

  • Remember login sessions
  • Store preferences and interface settings
  • Collect anonymous usage data via analytics tools (like Google Analytics)

Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored using encryption and server-side protection. We take all reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized access or misuse of your information.

Third-Party Services

We may integrate third-party services (like AI APIs, Google or Facebook login). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Trusted service providers who help operate the platform
  • Law enforcement if legally required

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Complain to the Information Regulator (South Africa)

Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time. This will remove all associated personal data from our systems, except where we are legally required to retain it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page and, if significant, we will notify you via email or in-app notification.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@studymate.ai.
