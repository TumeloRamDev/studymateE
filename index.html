Why StudyMate?

StudyMate was built to solve a growing problem among students and educators—disorganization, lack of motivation, and scattered study tools. In today’s fast-paced academic environment, students often struggle to keep track of their study material, assignments, quiz preparation, and meaningful academic progress. That’s where StudyMate steps in.

What is StudyMate?

StudyMate is your AI-powered social study assistant. It helps you upload and summarize your notes, track your study time and progress, connect with classmates, tutors, and lecturers, and even participate in community events and academic challenges—all in one secure, organized platform.

Who is it for?

  • Students: Stay focused, summarize notes, track progress, and study smarter.
  • Lecturers: Upload course content, monitor student engagement, and share resources.
  • Tutors: Help students directly through the Tutor Portal, share knowledge, and host sessions.

How Does StudyMate Benefit You?

  • ✅ All your study documents and notes in one place
  • ✅ Personalized study tracking and quiz scoring
  • ✅ AI summaries and explanations to simplify any topic
  • ✅ Connect with your academic community
  • ✅ Join study challenges and track leaderboards
